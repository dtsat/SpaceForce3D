SPACEFORCE3D

Standalone in the Builds folder. Simply run and enjoy!

Controls

Arrows to move
Space to shoot

Secrets:
U to recieve powerup
P to take damage

Avoid the ground, destroy all enemies in a wave to recieve bonus multiplier.
Boss' hands must be destroyed before it will take damage.
BEWARE: Boss special attack can destroy you in one hit no matter how many lives you have!
